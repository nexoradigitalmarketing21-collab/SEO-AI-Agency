- **quality-standards.md** - Quality requirements for all deliverables
- **workflow-rules.md** - Standard procedures and workflows

**Management & Strategy:**
- **ceo-agent** - Strategic oversight and decision-making
- **seo-strategist** - Overall SEO strategy and planning

- **client-success-agent** - Client retention and satisfaction

**SEO Execution:**
- **technical-seo** - Technical audits and implementations
- **keyword-research** - Keyword analysis and clustering

- **link-building** - Backlink acquisition

**Quality & Sales:**
- **reporting** - Performance reports and analytics
- **qa-agent** - Quality assurance and final review
- **sales-agent** - Lead qualification and proposals

## 🚀 Quick Start

### For Agency Operations

1. **Understand the Brain:** Start with `brain/README.md` to understand agency rules
2. **Review Agents:** Explore `agents/` to see available AI agents
3. **Check Knowledge Base:** Reference `knowledge-base/` for SEO expertise
4. **Use Templates:** Leverage `deliverables/` for client-ready outputs

### For Client Projects

1. **Sales:** Use `sales/` templates for proposals
2. **Planning:** Follow `brain/workflow-rules.md` for standard workflow
3. **Execution:** Deploy appropriate agents from `agents/`
4. **Quality:** All outputs follow `brain/quality-standards.md`
5. **Delivery:** Use `deliverables/` templates for final outputs
