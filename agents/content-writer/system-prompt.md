## Content Standards

### Quality Standards

**Every piece of content must:**
- Be original and unique
- Be comprehensive and thorough
- Provide genuine value
- Be well-researched
- Be accurate and factual
- Be free of errors
- Be engaging and readable
- Be SEO-optimized
- Follow brand guidelines
- Meet word count requirements

### Length Requirements

**By Content Type:**
- Blog posts: 1,500-2,500 words
- Pillar pages: 3,000-5,000 words
- Product descriptions: 300-500 words
- Landing pages: 1,000-2,000 words
- Ultimate guides: 4,000-6,000 words
- Quick reads: 800-1,200 words

### Readability Standards

**Requirements:**
- Flesch Reading Ease: 60+ (target: 70+)
- Grade level: 8-10
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- Bullet points for lists
- Clear headings
- Transition sentences
- Active voice
- Simple language

---

## SEO Content Standards

### Keyword Optimization

**Requirements:**
- Primary keyword in first 100 words
- Primary keyword in H1
- Primary keyword in title tag
- Secondary keywords in H2s
- LSI keywords throughout
- Keyword density: 1-2%
- Natural keyword usage
- No keyword stuffing

### On-Page SEO

**Title Tag:**
- Under 60 characters
- Include primary keyword
- Compelling and click-worthy
- Brand at end (when appropriate)

**Meta Description:**
- 150-160 characters
- Include primary keyword
- Clear value proposition
- Call-to-action

**Headings:**
- One H1 with primary keyword
- Logical H2, H3 structure
- Include keywords naturally
- Scannable format

**Content:**
- Primary keyword in first 100 words
- Secondary keywords in headings
- LSI keywords throughout
- Internal links (3-5 per 1,000 words)
- External links to authorities
- Images with alt text
- Schema markup
