## Search Intent Analysis

### Intent Categories

**Informational (40-50%):**
- User wants to learn or understand
- Examples: "what is SEO", "how to optimize"
- Content type: Blog posts, guides, tutorials
- Conversion path: Awareness → Consideration

**Commercial (20-30%):**
- User wants to compare or evaluate
- Examples: "best SEO tools", "SEO vs PPC"
- Content type: Comparisons, reviews, listicles
- Conversion path: Consideration → Decision

**Transactional (20-30%):**
- User wants to buy or purchase
- Examples: "buy SEO software", "SEO services pricing"
- Content type: Product pages, pricing pages
- Conversion path: Decision → Purchase

**Navigational (5-10%):**
- User wants to find specific page/site
- Examples: "Google Search Console", "Ahrefs login"
- Content type: Brand pages, login pages
- Conversion path: Direct access

### Intent Analysis Process

**For Each Keyword:**
1. Analyze search query
2. Review SERP results
3. Identify content types ranking
4. Determine user intent
5. Classify intent category
6. Map to buyer journey
7. Suggest content type

## Keyword Prioritization

### Priority Matrix

**Tier 1: High Priority (Target Immediately)**
- Criteria:
  - High search volume (1,000+)
  - Medium difficulty (30-50)
  - Clear business value
  - Aligns with business goals
  - Quick win potential
- Action: Create content immediately
- Timeline: 0-3 months

**Tier 2: Medium Priority (Target Within 6 Months)**
- Criteria:
  - Medium search volume (100-1,000)
  - Medium difficulty (30-60)
  - Good business value
  - Supports business goals
- Action: Plan and create content
- Timeline: 3-6 months

**Tier 3: Long-Tail (Target Within 12 Months)**
- Criteria:
  - Low search volume (< 100)
  - Low difficulty (< 40)
  - Niche relevance
  - Long-term value
- Action: Create as supporting content
- Timeline: 6-12 months

### Prioritization Factors

**1. Search Volume**
- Higher volume = more potential traffic
- Consider growth trends
- Look for rising keywords

**2. Keyword Difficulty**
- Lower difficulty = easier to rank
- Balance volume vs difficulty
- Consider domain authority

**3. Business Value**
- Alignment with business goals
- Conversion potential
- Revenue impact
- Strategic importance

**4. Intent Match**
- Transactional > Commercial > Informational
- Match to business model
- Consider buyer journey

**5. Feasibility**
- Can we create quality content?
- Do we have expertise?
- Resource requirements
- Timeline constraints
