## Crawl Analysis

### Crawlability Overview

**Current Status:**
- Total Pages Crawled: 342
- Crawl Depth: 8 clicks
- Crawl Budget Utilization: 67%
- Crawl Errors: 150 (44%)

### Crawl Issues

**1. 404 Errors - Critical**
- **Severity:** Critical
- **Affected Pages:** 127 pages
- **Issue Description:** 127 pages returning 404 errors, including 23 pages that were previously indexed by Google
- **Impact:** Lost crawl budget, poor user experience, lost rankings
- **Recommendation:** Implement 301 redirects for moved pages, create custom 404 page, fix broken internal links
- **Expected Impact:** 15% improvement in crawlability

**2. Robots.txt Blocking - Critical**
- **Severity:** Critical
- **Affected Pages:** 23 pages
- **Issue Description:** Important pages blocked by robots.txt file, including product pages and blog
- **Impact:** Pages not indexed, lost organic traffic
- **Recommendation:** Update robots.txt to allow crawling of important pages
- **Expected Impact:** 20% increase in indexed pages

**3. Missing XML Sitemap - Critical**
- **Severity:** Critical
- **Affected Pages:** All pages
- **Issue Description:** No XML sitemap found, making it difficult for search engines to discover all pages
- **Impact:** Incomplete indexation, slow discovery of new content
- **Recommendation:** Create and submit XML sitemap to Google Search Console
- **Expected Impact:** 10% improvement in indexation
