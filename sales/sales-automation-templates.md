# Sales Automation Templates

## Purpose

This document provides ready-to-use templates for the Sales Agent to automate and streamline the sales process across all freelance platforms.

**All templates are designed to be:**
- Professional and compelling
- Quick to customize
- Platform-optimized
- Conversion-focused
- Brand-consistent

### 4. Discovery Questions

#### Template 4A: Initial Discovery Call Questions

**Purpose:** Understand client needs and qualify the lead

**Questions:**

**1. Business Understanding:**
- "Can you tell me about your business and what you do?"
- "Who is your target audience?"
- "What makes your business unique?"

**2. Current Situation:**
- "What is your current online presence?"
- "Do you have a website? If yes, what platform?"
- "Are you currently doing any SEO?"
- "What's your current organic traffic?"

**3. Goals & Objectives:**
- "What are your main business goals?"
- "What do you want to achieve with SEO?"
- "What does success look like to you?"
- "What's your timeline for achieving these goals?"

**4. Budget & Resources:**
- "What's your budget for SEO services?"
- "Do you have an in-house marketing team?"
- "Who would I be working with?"
- "What's your decision-making process?"

**5. Pain Points:**
- "What challenges are you currently facing?"
- "What have you tried before?"
- "Why didn't it work?"
- "What's your biggest frustration?"

**6. Competition:**
- "Who are your main competitors?"
- "What are they doing well?"
- "What are they not doing?"
- "How do you differentiate?"

**7. Success Metrics:**
- "How will you measure success?"
- "What KPIs are most important to you?"
- "What results would make you happy?"
- "What would make this investment worth it?"

**8. Next Steps:**
- "What's your timeline for making a decision?"
- "Who else needs to be involved?"
- "What information do you need from me?"
- "When can we follow up?"

## Best Practices

### 1. Personalization
- Always use the client's name
- Reference specific details from their request
- Show you understand their unique needs
- Avoid generic, copy-paste responses

### 2. Speed
- Respond within 1-2 hours on platforms
- Follow up within 24-48 hours
- Be prompt with updates
- Meet your timelines

### 3. Value
- Lead with benefits, not features
- Show results with metrics
- Provide social proof
- Demonstrate expertise

### 4. Clarity
- Be clear about what you offer
- Set realistic expectations
- Explain your process
- Make next steps obvious

### 5. Professionalism
- Use proper grammar and spelling
- Maintain professional tone
- Be respectful of their time
- Follow through on commitments

## Customization Tips

### Make Templates Your Own

1. **Add your voice:** Adjust tone to match your brand
2. **Include your results:** Add your specific achievements
3. **Customize examples:** Use your actual case studies
4. **Update regularly:** Keep templates current
5. **Test and optimize:** A/B test different versions

### Platform Optimization

**Upwork:**
- Keep proposals under 400 words
- Use bullet points for scannability
- Include portfolio links
- Address client by name
- Reference specific requirements

**Fiverr:**
- Use emojis sparingly
- Highlight key benefits
- Include clear packages
- Add FAQ section
- Use bullet points

**Freelancer.com:**
- Be more formal
- Include detailed approach
- Show understanding of project
- Provide timeline
- Be specific about deliverables
