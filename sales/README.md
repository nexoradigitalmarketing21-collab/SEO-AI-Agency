## Sales Process

### Step 1: Opportunity Identification

**Monitor Platforms:**
- Upwork job invitations
- Fiverr buyer requests
- Freelancer.com projects
- LinkedIn inquiries
- Direct emails

**Qualification Criteria:**
- Budget alignment
- Project fit
- Timeline compatibility
- Client quality
- Win probability

### Step 2: Research & Strategy

**Client Research:**
- Review client profile
- Understand their business
- Identify pain points
- Review their website
- Research competitors
- Find connection points

**Strategy Development:**
- Identify key differentiators
- Select relevant case studies
- Determine pricing approach
- Plan proposal structure
- Prepare for objections

### Step 3: Proposal Creation

**Proposal Elements:**
- Personalized greeting
- Understanding of needs
- Proposed solution
- Relevant experience
- Clear pricing
- Strong CTA

**Platform Optimization:**
- Follow character limits
- Use platform best practices
- Include keywords
- Make scannable
- Mobile-friendly

### Step 4: Submission & Follow-Up

**Submission:**
- Submit on time
- Double-check for errors
- Follow platform guidelines
- Include all requirements
- Document submission

**Follow-Up:**
- Follow up appropriately
- Answer questions promptly
- Provide additional info
- Address concerns
- Build relationship

### Step 5: Closing & Handoff

**Closing:**
- Ask for the sale
- Create urgency
- Simplify next steps
- Make it easy to say yes
- Confirm agreement

**Handoff:**
- Introduce Project Manager
- Transfer context
- Set expectations
- Begin onboarding
- Start relationship

## Common Objections & Responses

### "Your price is too high"

**Response:**
- Acknowledge concern
- Explain value proposition
- Show ROI calculation
- Compare to alternatives
- Offer payment options
- Emphasize quality

### "Can you do it for less?"

**Response:**
- Understand budget constraints
- Offer reduced scope
- Suggest payment plan
- Show value justification
- Don't undervalue work
- Maintain profitability

### "Why should I choose you?"

**Response:**
- Highlight unique strengths
- Show relevant experience
- Provide case studies
- Demonstrate expertise
- Build trust
- Show commitment

### "I need it done quickly"

**Response:**
- Understand urgency
- Assess feasibility
- Explain timeline
- Offer rush options
- Ensure quality
- Set expectations
