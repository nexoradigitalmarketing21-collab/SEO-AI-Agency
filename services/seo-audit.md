# SEO Audit Service

## Purpose

The SEO Audit service provides a comprehensive analysis of a website's current SEO performance, identifying strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats. This service delivers actionable recommendations to improve search engine visibility and organic traffic.

**This service is the foundation for all SEO strategies** - understanding where you are before determining where to go.

---

## Service Overview

### What is an SEO Audit?

A systematic examination of a website to identify technical issues, content gaps, optimization opportunities, and competitive positioning. The audit provides a roadmap for improving search engine performance.

### Service Goals

- Identify technical SEO issues
- Analyze content performance
- Evaluate on-page optimization
- Assess off-page factors
- Provide actionable recommendations
- Create implementation roadmap

### Target Clients

- New clients needing SEO foundation
- Existing clients seeking improvement
- Websites with declining traffic
- Pre-launch website analysis
- Competitive analysis needs
- Strategic planning requirements

## Common Client Questions

### Q: How long does an SEO audit take?
**A:** Typically 10 business days for a comprehensive audit. Smaller websites may be completed in 7 days, while larger sites may take 14-21 days.

### Q: What's included in the audit?
**A:** A comprehensive audit includes technical SEO analysis, on-page SEO review, content analysis, off-page analysis, competitor analysis, and a detailed implementation roadmap.

### Q: Will the audit fix our SEO issues?
**A:** The audit identifies issues and provides recommendations. Implementation of fixes is a separate service, but we offer implementation support as an add-on.

### Q: How often should we do an SEO audit?
**A:** We recommend a comprehensive audit annually, with quarterly mini-audits to track progress and identify new issues.

### Q: What makes your audit different?
**A:** Our audits are comprehensive, actionable, and focused on delivering ROI. We don't just identify issues - we provide clear implementation guidance and prioritize by impact.
